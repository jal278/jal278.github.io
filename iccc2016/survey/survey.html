Rank images

In each of the following questions, you will be presented with three images of computer-generated objects and a category name. Please carefully look at each object to judge how well it could be an example of the category. Note that a category name could include multiple phrases that describe the same concept, e.g. "plunger, plumber's helper."

You will then be asked to rank the images by how well they resemble the category, where rank 1 is for the image that most resembles the category, and rank 3 is for the image that least resembles the category.

Thanks for your help! Your survey answers will contribute to the results of a scientific paper.
